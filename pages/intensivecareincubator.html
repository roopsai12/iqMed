THERMAL MANAGEMENT

Intensive Care Incubator

Preterm babies have a pretty hard time holding their body temperature stable. Zeal's Intensive Care Incubator is designed in such a way that it provides a warm and moist environment that helps the baby with minimal body heat loss and promotes better recovery. An isolated atmosphere very much suitable for Low Birth Weight and Very Low Birth Weight infants.

Overview / Highlights

  • ✔ An Isolated atmosphere for LBW and VLBW infants
  • ✔ Ergonomic and Intuitive User Interface
  • ✔ Acrylic double walled canopy
  • ✔ Bed Head Up/Down facility
  • ✔ Pull out Sliding Baby Tray
