About Us

Our Mission +

At IQ, our mission is to provide accessible and equitable Maternal and Neonatal care through innovative solutions and fostering partnerships with healthcare providers.

Our Vision +

At IQ, our vision is to be the global beacon of pioneering maternal and neonatal healthcare, driving innovations that redefine the standards of well-being for mothers and newborns.

Our Professional Team +

Assists healthcare providers in analyzing and choosing the right products along with successful installation, training, and maintenance.

Our Product Range +

Neonatal & Pediatric Intensive Care Equipment like Radiant Heat Warmers, Neonatal Resuscitation Units, Intensive Care Infant Incubators, High-Intensity LED Phototherapy Systems, Non-Invasive Ventilation System, Smart Interfaces & Consumables, Resuscitation Equipment, Resuscitation Accessories & Kits, etc.

Mr. Ayyappa.Punumalli

Founder

Mr. Ayyappa.Punumalli, qualified as a Biomedical Engineer, started his career in 2012 by servicing biomedical equipment for a few years and identified a gap in the field of neonatology equipment in India. He grabbed this opportunity and founded IQ Medical Systems Pvt. Ltd in 2012. He has been dedicated to continuous design and development of neonatal care equipment, producing cost-effective and reliable Infant Care Systems.

These are widely used in Government, Non-Government, Military Hospitals, Medical Colleges, Trust Hospitals, Private Clinics, etc., throughout India. Through continuous feedback and appreciation from renowned neonatologists, IQ has built its brand value in India with quality, consistency, and goodwill.

Mrs. Bhargavi.Tirupathi

Managing Director

Mrs. Bhargavi.Tirupathi, a qualified Computer Science Engineer, started her career as a director at IQ in 2016. Her ground-level experience helped her in spearheading the introduction of new products and widening the range of IQ Medical product offerings.

She has been instrumental in promoting the concept of Non-invasive ventilator products in India since 2016. Her achievements and focus on building customer relations globally drive IQ Medical to achieve its vision and mission of being a “One Stop One Solution” in neonatology.
