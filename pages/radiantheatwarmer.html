Radiant Heat Warmer

Easy to operate Radiant Heat Warmer is designed to deliver optimal thermal microenvironment for the neonates required in the delivery room, emergency, and NICU. Infrared ceramic heater elements and parabolic reflectors provide even heat distribution over the entire bed area.
