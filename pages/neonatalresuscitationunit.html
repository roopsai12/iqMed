THERMAL MANAGEMENT

Neonatal Resuscitation Unit

Neonatal Resuscitation Unit has been designed to deliver optimal thermal microenvironment for the neonates along with suction equipment, oxygenation and resuscitation support required in the delivery room, emergency and NICU. Infrared ceramic heater elements and parabolic reflectors provide even heat distribution over the entire bed area. So, whenever you place the baby on the bed, your little patient will always feel cozy and relaxed. The heater output is regulated by servo-control to keep the skin temperature constant at the site on the abdomen where a thermistor probe is attached.
