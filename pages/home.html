OUR STORY
IQ Medical Systems

IQ Medical Systems is an ISO 13485 certified organization. We value highest production standards, uncompromising quality of materials and component parts with attractive design. We are a globally oriented enterprise aiming at satisfaction of the customer's need all over India. Our enterprises have exported its products to many states across the country.

The product line of IQ Medical System is the result from decades of experience in neonatal care equipment. It has been developed as low-cost alternative for babies who require high quality care for Resuscitation, Thermal Care, hyperbilirubinemia management and Non-Invasive ventilation support. We focus on patients' needs with a user-friendly system.
