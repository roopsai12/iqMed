THERMAL MANAGEMENT

Transport Incubator

There are some situations in which a neonate needs to be transferred from one place to another by road, either in search of an intensive care unit or in another hospital for another therapy. Under this circumstance the baby is very sensitive to external temperature gradient environment. Zeal’s Transport Incubator is relatively light in weight and small which helps in easy movement of device. A baby is covered by an acrylic structure to provide close environment consist of port holes and small openings to insert Oxygen tubes, sensors etc. This device also works in the presence of external power source (which is mounted on transport incubator trolley) or on electricity derived from ambulance which makes the device works when it is moved.
